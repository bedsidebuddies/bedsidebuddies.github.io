About Bedside Buddies

Bedside Buddies is a volunteer program designed to provide companionship and emotional support to children experiencing extended hospital stays. We match college-age volunteers with pediatric patients to create meaningful connections that brighten their hospital experience.

Our volunteers engage children in age-appropriate activities like reading, playing games, doing crafts, or simply having conversations - providing a welcome distraction from medical treatments and hospital routines.

How Bedside Buddies Works

1

Apply & Train

College students complete an application and undergo specialized training for working with hospitalized children.

2

Get Matched

Volunteers are carefully matched with children based on interests, personality, and availability.

3

Weekly Visits

Volunteers visit their "buddy" weekly, engaging in fun activities and building a supportive relationship.

4

Make an Impact

Through consistent companionship, volunteers help reduce stress and bring joy to children's hospital stays.
